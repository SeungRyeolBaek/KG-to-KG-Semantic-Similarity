= why does it hurt so bad = " why does it hurt so bad " is a song recorded by american singer whitney houston for the 1995 film waiting to breathe . it was released on november 11 , 1996 , by columbia records as the third and final single from the film breathe . the song was written and produced solely by babyface . musically , it is an r & b song , and the lyrics describe a troubled relationship . the song received positive reviews from critics , who praised houston ' s solo effort . it charted in the united states on the billboard hot 100 , peaking at number twenty @ - @ three . it also reached a peak position of number twenty @ - @ three on the hot r & b / hip @ - @ pop songs chart and number three on the adult contemporary chart . in canada , the song reached a peak of number twenty @ - @ three on the rpm singles chart . although there was no official music video for the song , a performance of the song at the 1996 mtv movie awards was filmed and was released as a video single . the song was also released as a single , from her my love of your life world tour ( 1999 ) , along with a few other songs . = = = = = houston appeared in the 1995 drama film waiting to breathe , directed by michael whittaker . although houston did not want to contribute to the film ' s soundtrack , when whittaker asked babyface to produce the soundtrack , she agreed to . babyface , houston and several other male @ - @ and female artists contributed songs for the soundtrack . the song was one of the first contributions to the soundtrack .
