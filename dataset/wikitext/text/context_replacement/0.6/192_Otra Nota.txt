= otra nota = otra nota ( english : one day ) is the debut album by american singer marc anthony that was released on september 25 , 1993 , by rmm records . produced by sergio george , it was the first album by anthony to record in salsa after beginning his career as a freestyle musician . development of the album began when anthony convinced rmm manager ralph mercado to record juan gabriel ' s " hasta que te conoci " in spanish after hearing it on the radio during a taxi ride . recorded on a low budget , the album peaked at no . 1 on the billboard tropical albums chart and at no . 1 on the billboard top latin albums chart . the album was well received by critics who praised george ' s production and anthony ' s unique spirit . it received two nominations for " best new artist " at the billboard latin music awards and the lo nuestro awards . the album produced three singles : " hasta que te conoci " , " palabras del alma " , and " si tu no me fueras " , all of which charted on the billboard hot latin tracks chart . as of 2002 , otra nota has sold over 500 @ , @ 000 copies . = = = = = = anthony began his music career in 1991 as a freestyle musician during which he was a backup singer for salsa bands such as menudo and the cuban connection . anthony also wrote songs for his childhood band , @ -
