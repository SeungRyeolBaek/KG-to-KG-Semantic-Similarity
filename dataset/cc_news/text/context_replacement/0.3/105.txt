sydney ( ap ) — the men ' s and women ' s finals of the twenty20 cricket world cup in 2020 will be played at the sydney cricket ground as cricket australia gives both tournaments equal billing . for the first time , the men ' s and women ' s tournaments will be played as stand - alone events in the same country . the 10 - team women ' s event will run from feb . 21 to march 2 and the 16 - team men ' s event from oct . 30 to nov . 2 . both finals of the women ' s tournament will be played in sydney while the men ' s semifinals will be split between the sydney cricket ground and adelaide oval . finals will also take place at the 100 , 000 - seat mcg , australia ' s national stadium and venue for the final of the 2015 women ' s one - day international asia cup , which attracted 93 , 000 fans . a total of 13 venues in eight australian cities — melbourne , sydney , adelaide , adelaide , geelong , hobart , perth and brisbane — will host matches during the 2018 tournament . the men ' s tournament has been expanded so that the previous super 10 stage now becomes the super 12 with eight automatic qualifiers , and the top eight teams from two four - team groups which will contest the final stage . thethe cricket australia is hoping the women ' s final will attract a world record crowd for a women ' s sports event after 100 , 000 attended the final of the women ' s 50 - overs world cup at lord ' s in england last year . the australian record for a women ' s sports event is 100 , 000 , which attended a cricket match in adelaide in 1929 . the world record is 90 , 185 for the women ' s fifa world cup final in 1999 . " australia is a sporting nation , with diverse and passionate fans who love cricket , " cricket australia chief executive nick hockey said . " we ' ve designed the world t20 , comprising stand - alone women ' s and men ' s events , to be more inclusive , more accessible and more connected than ever before so that everyone can join in . " the australian women ' s team has won four of the last five twenty20 world cups and will be attempting to take back the twenty20 title they lost to the west indies in 2016 . the west indies are also the current men ' s champions .
