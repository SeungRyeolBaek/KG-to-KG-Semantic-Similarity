Mr. Hiroshi Ishino resigned from the position of Representative Director in KANSAI PAINT CO., LTD. effective June 27, 2019 . He joined the Company in March 2003. His previous titles include Senior Managing Director, Deputy Director of Paint Materials Business, Chief Director of Global Supply, Chief Director of International Affairs, Senior Managing Executive Officer and Manager of Product Planning Office in the Company. Prior to joining the Company, he worked for Mitsubishi Corporation. He obtained his LLB's degree from The University of Tokyo in March 1975.

Mr. Kunishi Mori was named President and Representative Director in KANSAI PAINT CO., LTD., effective April 1, 2019. He joined the Company in April 1981. He previously served as Chief Director of Sales Planning in the Company.
