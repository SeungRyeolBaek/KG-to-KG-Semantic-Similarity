diamond foods inc . completed the acquisition of oregon - born kettle foods , the san francisco based company announced today . under the terms of the agreement , diamond paid kettle ' s oregon - based parent , lion capitals llc , $ 615 million in cash for the snack food operation founded in salem . kettle ' s all - natural chips , nut , and flavor mix are distributed throughout north america , hawaii , guam and eastern europe . it had sales of $ 235 million in 2008 , according to lion capitals web site . the transaction was financed with proceeds from diamond ' s recent common stock offering , borrowings under a new 10 - year $ 600 million credit line , and additional cash flows . " the acquisition of kettle foods greatly strengthens our position in the food industry , " michael j . sullivan , chairman and ceo of diamond foods , said today . " kettle has become an icon in the organic , natural potato chip market and is a brand which has experienced tremendous growth . we look forward to investing in the kettle brand to further expand its footprint . " diamond foods has focused on acquiring and building food brands that also include kettle .
