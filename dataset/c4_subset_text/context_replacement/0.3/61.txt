smartphones can play a very important role as a new tool with ophthalmologists , shows a new study conducted by the emory university . smartphones can be easily used by ophthalmologists for viewing complex inner eye photos for diagnostic purposes . they can also be used for viewing , recording and analyzing pictures of the damage to the front of the eye or eyelids . the study was conducted among about 350 patients who reported problems with vision , eyesight , and similar vision problems in operating rooms . the study also included the inner - eye photos taken by the er doctor using an ocular camera . then the study also included the response of two doctors who viewed and rated the pictures on a tablet pc and did the same on an iphone . the results of the study , published in the archives of medicine , show that the doctors found the iphone images better or better than pc photos and rated them high . while one doctor found 53 images were of the same quality , another found 46 better on iphone and just one was better on pc . for the other ophthalmologist ;
