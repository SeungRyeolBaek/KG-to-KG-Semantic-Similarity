= = highway = the hope highway , also known as the hope river segment , is a forest highway located in the kenai peninsula borough , in the u . s . state of alaska . the highway connects the city of hope to the hope river , and travels through 17 miles ( 27 km ) of the chugach national forest . the road passes the ghost town of sunrise city and several ghost towns , remnants of the gold rush that occurred in that area . the highway was created circa 1928 and is designated as forest highway 14 by the federal highway administration . = = route description = = the hope highway begins at an intersection with the hope highway ( ak @ -
