although the low became disorganized over the southwestern gulf of mexico due to increased wind shear . however , the wind shear decreased , and early on september 25 , another hurricane hunters flight reported the development of a closed , @ - @ type circulation . based on that observation , along with sufficient persistence of the system , the system developed into tropical depression eleven about 190 mi ( 305 km ) east of veracruz , tamaulipas . upon formation , the depression was located in an area of weak steering currents , resulting in an erratic movement generally to the south . in its early stages , the depression executed a small loop . as it did so , it moved into an area of relatively warm waters and low wind shear . the system ' s convection gradually organized , although the winds were slower to develop . an eye became established over the system ,and the depression intensified into tropical storm lorenzo at around 1200 utc on september 26 . at the time , it was located about 150 mi ( 240 km ) east of tuxpan , veracruz , moving generally westward under the influence of a subtropical ridge to the east . as it approached the coast , lorenzo rapidly intensified further , and within 12 hours of attaining tropical storm status , intensified into a hurricane . hurricane lorenzo quickly developed a large eyewall , which was visible on radar and had a maximum wind estimate of 80 mph ( 130 km / h ) by 0000 utc on september 27 . the storm maintained deep and symmetric convection around the center , although the structure weakened slightly . lorenzo crossed the coast near tecolutla , veracruz at 0500 utc that day , with winds of around 75 mph ( 120 km / h ) . after making landfall , the storm rapidly weakened to tropical storm intensity . progressing inland , the circulation became difficult to locate as the convection diminished to rainbands along the coast . within 19 hours of moving ashore , the circulation of lorenzo dissipated , effectively ending its existence as a tropical cyclone . = = preparations and impact = = about 26 hours before landfall , the mexican government issued a tropical storm watch from palma sola to santa
