= the remix ( lady gaga remix ) = the remix is a remix album by american recording artist lady gaga . released in japan on may 3 , 2010 , it contains remixes of the songs from her first studio album , the fame ( 2008 ) , and her first extended play , the fame remixes ( 2009 ) . a revised version of the track list was announced for release in other markets , beginning with mexico on may 3 , 2010 . a number of other artists have remixed the album , including pet shop boys , passion pit and the sound of arrows . the remixed songs are more uptempo and downtempo compositions , with altered lyrics from gaga . the album received mixed reviews from music critics , although many praised how gaga was able to sell the songs from the album in new and novel ways . it reached the top of charts in greece and the dance / electronic albums chart of billboard 200 in the united states . in other nations , the remix charted within the top twenty in belgium ( flanders and wallonia regions ) , canada , brazil , france , mexico , the united kingdom and the billboard 200 chart of united states , while reaching the top @ -
